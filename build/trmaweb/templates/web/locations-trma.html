Two Rivers Martial Arts has seven locations in central Iowa. Classes are taught at each of these locations by TRMA black belt instructors. Advanced students from all branches attend Brown/Black Belt Class Friday nights at 6:00 PM, and Sundays at 3:00 PM at the Two Rivers Hub location.


Brown/Black Belt Class is longer than a typical class and offers experienced students an opportunity to work together on advanced techniques. During this class students spend more time on free sparring and self-defense techniques than is possible during a typical class.
