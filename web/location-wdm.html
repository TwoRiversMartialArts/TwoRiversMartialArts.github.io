Two Rivers Martial Arts is a non-profit organization. All instructors are unpaid volunteers, which allows us to keep prices low. Our instructors are all black belts, some of which have over 20 years of experience. A student enrolled at any branch can attend as many classes as desired at any other branch with no additional charge. The expectation is that each student choose a primary branch and instructor who will guide the student's preparations for promotion tests.

Payment Options

There are several options for paying class fees. Two Rivers recommends setting up a recurring payment using an online bill-pay service or another automated method. This helps instructors focus their time on teaching. When paying by mail or electronically, please include an account number or student names. See the contact page for ways to reach the Hub business office to request your account number, provide feedback, or ask any questions. Your instructor can also obtain your account number from the Two Rivers treasurer. Options for payment:

  • Online bill pay - Have your bank send payments to the Hub business office address ( see the contact page ). It will also be helpful to send a note to the Hub office, letting us know you've set this up.


  • Credit/Debit card - The Hub business office, and some individual instructors are equipped to accept credit or debit card payments using mobile swiper equipment.


  • Cash/Check - Instructors will accept cash or checks made out to the school. Checks can also be mailed to the Hub business office. Include in the check memo what the payment covers ( class fees, boards, uniform, etc. ) and student name.


  • Paypal - Use the Paypal address. Please include a comment with the branch name, student name(s), month(s), or other info explaining the payment.


  • At Tests - The Hub business office is staffed on test days and is able to accept payments from students of any branch for class fees, boards, or any other items.


  • Other - If there are other payment methods that students want to use, talk to your instructor, or send a message to trmaweb@tworiversmartialarts.com. Two Rivers will work to enable new payment options if there is interest from students.

Class fee payments are due at the beginning of each month. Some students prefer to pay on a 2-month cycle aligning with our scheduled promotion tests, which works as well.


Use these buttons to pay with Paypal or credit/debit card:


Select the items you wish to pay for, and on the shopping cart page, you can select a quantity for each. For class fees, The quantity value determines the number of months you'd like to pay for. If paying for one session at a time, set the quantity value to 2. You can pay for as many months as you like.


Signing up for a subscription requires a Paypal account, to manage the subscription, and will charge the same fees on a monthly basis. If you don't have a Paypal account, you can create one during the checkout process.

Testing, Promotion, and Test Fees

Promotion tests are held every other month. No one is required to test, but testing is required to achieve higher rank and learn new techniques. Students must meet certain requirements in order to test. See instructor for details. The test fee for white through brown belt levels is $45 per student. Test fees apply to the first three members in a family. Additional family members do not pay test fees. Please talk to your instructor for information about fees related to testing for temporary black belt rank and above. Testing schedule and requirements for temporary black belt tests are discussed on the curriculum page.
