Related Schools



The following schools are related to Two Rivers Martial Arts. Some of these schools are run by former students of Master Heintz. Others are run by Masters or Grand Masters who have long realtionships with Master Heintz. What all of these academies have in common is a commitment to training families in the traditional style of Korean Tae Kwon Do. All of these schools are members of the American Black Belt Association and practice both the ITF and WTF forms. All of these schools emphasize the importance of the Tenets of Tae Kwon Do:

  • Courtesy
  • Integrity
  • Perseverence
  • Self Control
  • Indomitable Spirit


The following schools have a style similar to, and maintain a close friendship with, Two Rivers Martial Arts. It is alphabetized by State (within the US) or by country. Each schools' name, location, and contact person is provided. Where available, Email and website links are also provided.
