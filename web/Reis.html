About Daniel Reis (Master)

Daniel Reis is a 7th degree black belt with more than 25 years of experience in a wide variety of martial arts and self-defense disciplines. He is a master instructor having taught many other instructors throughout the years. He has won 36 grand champion competitions in forms and point sparring and over a hundred 1st place. He teaches Tae Kwon Do, Kobudo, and eskrima weapons, as well as practical street defense, womens' self-defense, pressure sensitive, joint manipulation and grappling. Mr. Reis specializes in training students of all ages (children as young as 4 through senior citizens). He can also customize his training programs to accommodate people with special needs and disabilities.
