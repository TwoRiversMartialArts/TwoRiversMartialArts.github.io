Calendar

This calendar shows special events, at TRMA and elsewhere of interest to our students. Regularly scheduled Tae Kwon Do, Martial Spirit, Tai Chi and Kobudo classes are not shown. The regular class schedules are listed on the branch locations page.


If you invite hub@trma.us to your calendar event, it will show up here once it is reviewed and accepted. The event doesn't have to occur at the Hub to be published on this calendar. Contact support@trma.us if you have any questions.
