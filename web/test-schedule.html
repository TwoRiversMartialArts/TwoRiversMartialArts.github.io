Test Schedule



Promotion tests are usually scheduled on the first Saturday of each even month. All tests are held at the Two Rivers Martial Arts Hub. If you have any questions about your test or times, please contact your instructor. December 10th, 2016 test times have been set as shown below.

The December 2016 tests have been rescheduled, and will be on December 10th rather than the 3rd. Please see the announcement.

Green Belts 9:00 am
Black Belt Demo 9:45 am
White Belts 10:15 am
Orange Belts 11:30 am
Yellow Belts 12:30 pm
Blue Belts 1:45 pm
Brown Belts 2:30 pm
