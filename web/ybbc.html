Young Black Belt Class



 
Two Rivers Martial Arts ‘Young Black Belt Club’ is open to all Black Belts age 19 and under. Classes usually meet on the 2nd Saturday* of each month from 1pm to 3pm at the Hub. Social time is scheduled after the class which will last until 4:30pm.

Each month a different Black Belt instructor will lead the class offering new insights on techniques, forms, one-steps, board breaking, teaching practices, self defense, history along with occasionally sampling other martial arts of which our senior Black Belts have participated in. Black Belts that attend this class are challenged both physically and mentally.




This is an invaluable opportunity for the Young Black Belts to interact with other Young Black Belts within our school and to have the opportunity to train with our senior instructors.

*Scheduling of the monthly meeting is sometimes changed to accomodate tournaments or other events. Please watch for the monthly announcements regarding meeting date and guest instructor.
