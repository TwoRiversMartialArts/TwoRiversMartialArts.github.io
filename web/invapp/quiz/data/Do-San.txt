Do-San

Low Orange

Pseudonym of the patriot Ahn Chang Ho (1876-1938) who devoted his life to furthering the education of Koreans and their independence movement.

24

1. Turn 90 degrees to the left into a left front stance while performing an outer forearm block with the left arm
2. Reverse middle section punch, Turn right 90 degrees to the right into a riding horse stance with left fist at side palm up and right fist palm down above it
3. Turn 90 degrees to the right into a right front stance while performing an outer forearm block with the right arm
4. Reverse middle section punch
5. Slide right foot back and left foot forward (shuffling both feet) while turning 90 degrees to the left into a right back stance, knife-hand guarding block middle
6. Step into a right front stance, kwansu and kiyap
7. Turn 360 degrees counter clockwise while pivoting on the right foot while moving right hand to small of back.  Land in a left front stance and perform a high backfist (temple strike)
8. Step forward into a right front stance, high backfist (temple strike)
9. Turn 270 degrees to the left (counter clockwise) into a left front stance while performing an outer forearm block with the left arm
10. Reverse middle section punch, Turn right 90 degrees to the right into a riding horse stance with left fist at side palm up and right fist palm down above it
11. Turn 90 degrees to the right into a right front stance while performing an outer forearm block with the right arm
12. Reverse middle section punch
13. Pick up left foot and turn left 135 degrees into a left front stance, twin fists high
14. Right front snap kick high landing in a right front stance
15. High punch
16. Reverse high punch
17. Pick up right foot and turn right 90 degrees into a front stance, twin fists high
18. Left front snap kick high landing in a left front stance
19. High punch
20. Reverse high punch
21. Pivot on right foot and turn 45 degrees to the right into a left front stance while executing a high block
22. Step forward with right foot into right front stance while executing a high block
23. Turn left 270 degrees (counter clockwise) while pivoting on right foot into a horse stance, left knife-hand strike
24. Slide left foot to the right, then slide right foot to the right into a horse stance, right knife-hand strike and kiyap
