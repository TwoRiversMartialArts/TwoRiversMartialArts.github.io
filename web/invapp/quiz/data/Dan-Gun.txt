Dan-Gun

Low Yellow

Named after the holy Dan-Gun, the legendary founder of Korea in the year 2333 B.C.

21

1. Turn 90 degrees into right back-stance while performing a knife-hand guarding block middle
2. Step forward with right foot into a right front stance and high punch
3. Turn 180 degrees into a left back stance while performing a knife-hand guarding block middle
4. Step forward with left foot into a left front stance and high punch
5. Turn 90 degrees into a left front stance and low block
6. Step forward into a right front stance and high punch
7. Step forward into a left front stance and high punch
8. Step forward into a right front stance, high punch and kiyap
9. Turn 270 degrees to the left (counter clockwise), pivoting on right foot, into a right back stance, twin forearm guarding block
10. Step forward into a right front stance and high punch
11. Turn 180 degrees to the right into a left back stance, twin forearm guarding block
12. Step forward into a left front stance and high punch
13. Turn 90 degrees to the left into a left front stance while low blocking.
14. While remaining in the same stance, rechamber and perform a left high block
15. Step forward into a right front stance, high block
16. Step forward into a left front stance, high block
17. Step forward into a right front stance, high block
18. Turn 270 degrees to the left, while pivoting on the right foot, into a right back stance and perform a knife-hand strike.  Right fist should be chambered at the waist.
19. Step forward into a right front stance, high punch
20. Turn 180 degrees to the right into a left back stance, knife hand strike.  Left fist should be chambered at the waist.
21. Step forward into a left front stance, high punch
