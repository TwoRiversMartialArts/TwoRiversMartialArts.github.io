Hub Branch

Grandmaster Steve
Gonzalez

8th Dan

Grandmaster Gonzalez started training with Master Heintz in the mid 1980s. He earned his 8th Dan in December 2016. He is one of the primary instructors at the TRMA Hub and is a current member and former President and Vice President of the TRMA Board of Directors. Grandmaster Gonzalez is the director of the annual Pil Sung tournament.

Deaton

8th Dan

7th Dan

Master Bryan
Siever

6th Dan

Master Siever began training with Master Heintz in the spring of 1993. He earned his 6th Dan in December of 2016. Master Siever is one of the primary instructors at the TRMA Hub as well a former President and a former Secretary of the TRMA Board.

Mr. Lance Kinseth

Mr. Kinseth began training with Master Heintz in 1983 and has taught taekwondo at various branches and at the Hub. He has over 45 years experience in a variety of martial arts, with particular emphasis upon taekwondo, kumdo and jodo. He is the instructor for the Martial Spirit class that integrates a broad variety of martial arts.
