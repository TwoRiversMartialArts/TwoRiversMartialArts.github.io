Primary Instructors



The following is a listing of the primary instructors at Two Rivers Martial Arts. These instructors may be contacted for further information about taking classes at their branches. Included below are the instructors' names, ranks, and some additional information. Photos have also been included where available.

Two Rivers Hub

(& Business Office)

 trmaweb@tworiversmartialarts.com
(515)423-0804
Grandmaster Steve Gonzalez
8th Dan
Grandmaster Gonzalez started training with Master Heintz in the mid 1980s. He earned his 8th Dan in December 2016. He is one of the primary instructors at the TRMA Hub and is a former President and Vice President of the TRMA Board of Directors.  Grandmaster Gonzalez is the director of the annual Pil Sung tournament.
Grandmaster Brad Deaton
8th Dan
Grandmaster Deaton began training with Master Heintz in 1986. Grandmaster Deaton earned his 8th Dan in December 2016. He is currently one of the primary instructors at the TRMA Hub. He is a current member of and former President and Vice-President of the TRMA Board of Directors.
Master Anita Williams
7th Dan
Master Williams began training with Master Heintz in 1987 and earned her 7th Dan in December 2016. She is one of the primary instructors at the TRMA Hub, is a member of the TRMA Board and is a former President and former Secretary of the TRMA Board.
Master Dwayne Ferguson
7th Dan
Master Ferguson began training with Master Heintz in 1989 and earned his 7th Dan in December 2016. He is one of the primary instructors at the TRMA Hub and is the current Secretary of the TRMA Board, and a former President and Treasurer of the TRMA Board of Directors.
Master Bryan Siever
6th Dan
Master Siever began training with Master Heintz in the spring of 1993. He earned his 6th Dan in December of 2016. Master Siever is one of the primary instructors at the TRMA Hub as well a former President and a former Secretary of the TRMA Board.
Mr. Lance Kinseth
 
Mr. Kinseth began training with Master Heintz in 1983 and has taught taekwondo at various branches and at the Hub. He has over 45 years experience in a variety of martial arts, with particular emphasis upon taekwondo, kumdo and jodo. He is the instructor for the Martial Spirit class that integrates a broad variety of martial arts.
Ms. Rochelle Douglass
3rd Dan
Ms. Douglass began training in 2005 with Iowa Methodist Tae Kwon Do.  Ms. Douglass earned her 3rd Dan in April 2015 through TRMA. She is one of the primary instructors at the TRMA Hub, is a member of the TRMA Board and is a past Vice-President of the TRMA Board.
Mr. John Hornback
3rd Dan
Mr. Hornback began training in TKD in 2009. He is currently 3rd Dan and one of the primary instructors at the Hub for the Black Belt Youth Group and has been since it was conceived in the spring of 2013. He is also the schools only instructor for Kobudo (Okinawan weapons) and earned a black belt in Kobudo in 2017. Mr. Hornback is a past member of the TRMA Board of Directors.
Mr. Dave Nelson
2nd Dan
Mr. Nelson started with Two Rivers Tae Kwon Do in August of 2010. He is currently a 2nd Dan Black Belt, and teaches at the TRMA Hub branch.
