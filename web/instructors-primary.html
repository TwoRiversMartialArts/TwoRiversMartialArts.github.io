Primary Instructors



The following is a listing of the primary instructors at Two Rivers Martial Arts. These instructors may be contacted for further information about taking classes at their branches. Included below are the instructors' names, ranks, and a some additional information. Photos have also been included where available.

Two Rivers Hub (& Business Office)
Master Judy Clinton
6th Dan
Master Clinton began training with Master Heintz in the early 1980s and was the primary instructor at the Clive Branch until its closure in 1997. She is a currently on the TRMA Board of Directors and a former President, Vice-President, and Secretary of the Board. She is one of the primary instructors at TRMA Hub. Master Clinton tested for 6th Dan in April 2008.
Master Steve Gonzalez
6th Dan
Master Gonzalez started training with Master Heintz in the mid 1980s. He earned his 6th Dan in April 2008. He is one of the primary instructors at the TRMA Hub and is a former President and Vice President of the TRMA Board of Directors.  Master Gonzalez is the director of the annual Pil Sung tournament.
Master Brad Deaton
6th Dan
Master Deaton began training with Master Heintz in 1986. Master Deaton earned his 6th Dan in August 2011. He is currently one of the primary instructors at the TRMA Hub. He is a current member of the Board of Directors and is a former President and former Vice-President of the Board of Directors.
Master Anita Williams
6th Dan
Master Williams began training with Master Heintz in 1987 and earned her 6th Dan in August 2011. She is one of the primary instructors at the TRMA Hub and is a current member and a former President and former Secretary of the TRMA Board.
Master Dwayne Ferguson
6th Dan
Master Ferguson began training with Master Heintz in 1989 and earned his 6th Dan in August 2011. He is one of the primary instructors at the TRMA Hub and is the current Treasurer and a former President of the TRMA Board of Directors.
Mr. Lance Kinseth
 
Mr. Kinseth began training with Master Heintz in 1983 and has taught taekwondo at various branches and at the Hub. He has over 45 years experience in a variety of martial arts, with particular emphasis upon taekwondo, kumdo and jodo. He is the instructor for the Martial Spirit class that integrates a broad variety of martial arts.
Mr. Bryan Siever
4th Dan
Mr. Siever began training with Master Heintz in the spring of 1993. He earned his 4th Dan in June of 2010. Mr. Siever is one of the primary instructors at the TRMA hub as well a former President, a former Secretary and a current member of the TRMA Board.
Mr. Bruce Brown
3rd Dan
Mr. Brown began training with Two Rivers in 2007 and earned his 3rd Dan in December 2013.  He is a former President and former Secretary of the TRMA Board of Directors.
Ms. Becky Hammar
2nd Dan
Mrs. Hammar is 2nd Dan and teaches on Thursday nights at the hub location.  She is a current member of the TRMA board of Directors.
